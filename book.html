Disclaimer: This is my ongoing attempt to translate the novel Di Viole E Liquirizia, by Nico Orengo, from Italian to English. I don't know much Italian, though I hope this improves it. I'm not a writer, and let's say I'm not at an optimal age for language acquisition. So far, I've used only Google Translate, other web resources, and some basic grammar books, so the result should be considered more of paraphrase than translation. It's full of mistranslations, both known and inadvertent, educated guesses, wild guesses, typos, grammatical errors and misspellings. Nuance and geographical accuracy are often lost in translation. Caveat lector.

The book is about a Parisian sommelier who comes to Alba, in the heart of the famous Langhe region in Piedmont. Caveats aside, if you're interested in wine, especially Italian wine, and particularly wines from Piedmont, you may enjoy it.
