Disclaimer: This is my ongoing attempt to translate the novel Di Viole E Liquirizia, by Nico Orengo, from Italian to English. I don't know much Italian, though I hope this improves it. Furthermore, I'm not a writer, and let's say I'm not at an optimal age for language acquisition. To do this (so far), I've used only Google Translate, other web resources, and some basic grammar books. The result should be considered more of a paraphrase than a translation. It is full of mistranslations, both known and inadvertent, educated guesses, wild guesses, typos, grammatical errors and misspellings. Nuance and geographical accuracy are often missing. Caveat lector.
